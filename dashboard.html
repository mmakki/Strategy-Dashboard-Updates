UTS 2030 — Four strategic priorities

Creative & innovative

Creativity & innovation in teaching, research & operations. Interdisciplinary collaboration. Research translation.

Student-centred

Inclusive pathways. Digital-first learning. Work-integrated learning. Ethical GenAI. Indigenous graduate attribute.

Connected & engaged

Industry & community partnerships. Knowledge Exchange. Global digital education.

Resilient & agile

Sustainable revenue. Staff capability. Operational efficiency. Cultural excellence.

Creative Industries — Five focus areas

FA1 — Reimagining creative education

Social, technological & material practice. Creative technologies, circular economy, climate leadership.

FA2 — Creative Village

Co-location of creative practitioners. Tech Central. Contemporary learning & research spaces.

FA3 — Research excellence

Decarbonisation, social justice, Indigenous knowledges. Visualisation, AR/VR.

FA4 — Indigenous self-determination

Cultural Resilience Hub. Indigenous data sovereignty. Truth-telling through creative practice.

FA5 — Partnerships for global impact

Industry-academia pipelines. International partnerships. Showcase programs.
