    Community of Practice · Teaching & Learning Strategy

    A collective direction

    The presented roadmap is a shared direction that examines experience, capability, connection and identity. It asks us to think about how students enter, move through and belong in the Faculty; how we prepare them to work creatively and critically in changing fields; how we embed ethical, cultural and community responsibilities into learning; and how we use our partnerships, disciplines, place and people to define what makes FDS distinct. More importantly, it gives us an opportunity to grow with greater clarity and confidence. By building from what we already do well, while being honest about what needs to change, we can shape a Faculty that stands out not by trying to be like others, but by being clearer about who we are, what we value, and the kind of learning experience only we can offer.
